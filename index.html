Trainings and Exercises

Consistent training and exercise are the building blocks for a strong body, sharp mind, and resilient spirit.

Forward Lunge : Strengthens the legs and improves balance.

Push-Up : Builds upper body strength, especially in the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Box Jump : Enhances explosive power and agility.

Side Lunge : Targets inner and outer thigh muscles while improving lateral stability.

Bodyweight Squat : Strengthens the lower body and core, improving overall muscle tone.

Sit-Ups : Strengthens the abdominal muscles, improving core stability.
